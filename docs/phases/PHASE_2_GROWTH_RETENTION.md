# Phase 2 Playbook: Growth and Retention

Timeline: Month 4 to Month 6
Primary Goal: Increase repeat bookings, reduce churn, and improve lifetime value.

## Business Outcomes

1. Repeat booking behavior becomes default.
2. Customer retention improves month-over-month.
3. Revenue predictability improves through loyalty mechanics.

## Scope

In scope:
- loyalty and rewards
- waitlist and cancellation fill
- rebooking shortcuts
- customer profile insights
- promo and referral mechanics

Out of scope:
- multi-barber marketplace rollout
- platform-level commission models

## Hybrid Model Execution

### Discovery Track

Tasks:
- identify retention drop-off points
- validate incentives by customer segment
- test referral messaging and reward economics

Deliverables:
- retention hypothesis backlog
- validated experiment plans

### Delivery Track

Tasks:
- ship growth features in measurable increments
- instrument all feature events
- tune experience based on cohort analytics

Deliverables:
- retention dashboard with feature attribution
- mature growth feature set

## Workstreams

### Workstream A: Retention Mechanics

Features:
- loyalty points with redemption rules
- rebook in one tap
- personalized reminders

### Workstream B: Demand Shaping

Features:
- waitlist for high-demand slots
- dynamic cancellation replacement notifications

### Workstream C: Acquisition Loops

Features:
- referral flows
- promo code lifecycle management

### Workstream D: Insight and Personalization

Features:
- customer history summaries for barber
- service preference modeling
- campaign segmentation inputs

## Measurement Framework

Primary metrics:
1. 30-day retention
2. repeat booking rate
3. referral conversion rate
4. average order value

Guardrail metrics:
1. discount leakage
2. cancellation increase
3. support ticket volume

## Exit Criteria

Phase 2 exits when:
1. repeat booking rate and retention hit target thresholds.
2. growth loops are measurable and financially sustainable.
3. loyalty and referral abuse controls are active.
4. operational dashboards are trusted and reviewed weekly.
