# Phase 4 Playbook: Marketplace and Monetization

Timeline: Month 12+
Primary Goal: Build durable revenue systems and expand geographically.

## Business Outcomes

1. Platform earns recurring, predictable revenue.
2. Monetization is aligned with customer and barber value.
3. Expansion is operationally controlled and compliant.

## Scope

In scope:
- commission and payout economics
- subscription plans and feature packaging
- boosted discovery placements
- product and voucher commerce extensions
- expansion readiness controls

Out of scope:
- ungoverned rapid expansion without operational maturity

## Hybrid Model Execution

### Discovery Track

Tasks:
- price sensitivity and willingness-to-pay analysis
- incentive alignment for barbers and customers
- expansion market readiness studies

Deliverables:
- monetization strategy per segment
- phased expansion playbook

### Delivery Track

Tasks:
- implement billing and entitlement systems
- integrate payout and commission logic
- launch monetization experiments under guardrails

Deliverables:
- monetization stack with reporting
- city expansion launch runbooks

## Monetization Streams

1. transaction commissions
2. subscription tiers for barber tools
3. listing boosts and promotions
4. product sales and gift cards
5. enterprise or B2B booking packages

## Financial and Compliance Controls

1. transparent fee calculations and statements.
2. payout reconciliation and exception handling.
3. tax and regulatory readiness by market.
4. fraud detection and abuse prevention in promotional systems.

## KPI Targets

1. monthly recurring revenue growth trend positive.
2. churn on paid plans below target threshold.
3. monetized barber adoption meets forecast.
4. payout error rate below agreed operational limit.

## Exit Criteria

Phase 4 milestones are considered achieved when:
1. at least two independent revenue streams are stable.
2. financial reporting and reconciliation are reliable.
3. expansion readiness gates pass for each new geography.
4. monetization does not materially reduce booking trust or conversion.
